How to Get to Vršac?

From Belgrade

Vršac is located about 85 km southeast of Belgrade. It is accessible by several means of transport:

  • By Car: Highway E-70 through Pančevo leads directly to Vršac. The drive takes around 1h 30min.
  • By Train: Direct railway line from Belgrade. Duration: about 2h. Train schedule.
  • By Bus: Departures from BAS. The journey takes around 2h.
From Novi Sad

Vršac is about 130 km southeast. Options include:

  • By Car: Via A1/E75 highway toward Belgrade, then toward Vršac. Total: 2h 30min.
  • By Bus (via Zrenjanin): First to Zrenjanin, then Zrenjanin–Vršac line.
  • Via Belgrade: First to Belgrade, then continue by train or bus to Vršac.
